My research focuses on the networks of online communities in the field of social computing. In contrast to managing effects of social networks post-impact, I focus on building network algorithms and structures that proactively create communities for healthy personal wellbeing and productive community discourse. Lately, I am particularly interested in how the structure of a user's network affects their mental/emotional health. My work includes network analysis, information retrieval, and applied machine learning.
