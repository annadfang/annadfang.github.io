Hi, I'm Anna!

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the University of Washington in Computer Science. I graduated from Cornell University with a BS in Computer Science in 2019 and worked as a software engineer at Amazon in Seattle.

I am broadly interested in social computing and human-computer interaction. My work explores how the networks of social and information systems can be leveraged to make people's lives healthy, productive, and fair, with applications to mental/emotional wellbeing, education and collaboration, and algorithmic fairness for disadvantaged groups.

I am (proudly) a Hoosier, born and raised in Indiana. I lived in Ithaca, New York to attend Cornell University, then Seattle, Washington to work at Amazon for a year. I have also lived summers in El Segundo, California and Indianapolis, Indiana.

Outside of academia, I am an avid artist with my mediums of choice being graphite pencil and acrylic paint. I was a competitive youth artist in Indiana for about a decade, although I no longer sell or compete.

Other things I love include: filmmaking, botany, coffee/tea, films (especially Wes Anderson), and my greyhound Milo.

Other fun facts: I'm an INFJ. I have a very high spice tolerance. I make a very good London Fog.. My birthmark is on my eye.
