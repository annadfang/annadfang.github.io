ANNA M. FANG

Hi, I'm Anna! My research focuses on human-computer interaction for mental, emotional, & social well-being. I will start as an assistant professor (i.e. Lecturer) at the University of Melbourne in the School of Computing & Information Systems in 2026.

I received my Ph.D. at Carnegie Mellon University in the School of Computer Science and Human-Computer Interaction Institute, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Haiyi Zhu in the Social AI Group. Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science at Cornell University, and spent a bit of time working as a software engineer at Amazon in Seattle, Washington.
