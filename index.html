Greyhound racing is often very cruel; many dogs are killed for losing races, break bones, or are retired and now in need of good homes. Greyhounds are known to be docile, lazy, calm, and sensitive by nature. They are fantastic for apartments as they are extremely quiet, sleep all day, and barely shed. Please consider adopting a greyhound if you are looking for a pet!
