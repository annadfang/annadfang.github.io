Selected Research:

My research is broad within the realm of mental health and well-being, spanning online communities, to simulation and experimentation systems, to VR/AR and embodied experiences. However, I am particularly interested in sociotechnical systems that enable community well-being (e.g. mental health, social relationships, productiveness) in proactive ways ‚Äî those that are self-sustaining, self-correcting, or promote positive behaviors ‚Äî rather than retrospective handling of harm.
