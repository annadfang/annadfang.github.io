Anna Fang

I am a first-year PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University in Human-Computer Interaction (School of Computer Science).

I am broadly interested in social computing. My work explores how the networks of social and information systems can be leveraged to make people's lives healthy, productive, and fair, with applications to mental/emotional wellbeing, education and collaboration, and algorithmic fairness for disadvantaged groups.

Previously, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Cornell University with a BS in Computer Science and was a software engineer at Amazon.
