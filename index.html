Many greyhounds are retired or rescued racers and now in need of good homes. Greyhounds are famously docile, lazy, and calm; retired racers were raised to be well-handled and polite. They are fantastic for apartments as they are extremely quiet, sleep all day, and barely shed. Please consider adopting a greyhound if you are looking for a dog!

My research is in social computing and focuses on the networks of online communities. In contrast to managing effects of social networks post-impact, I strive to build network algorithms and structures that proactively create communities for healthy personal wellbeing and productive community discourse. Lately, I am particularly interested in how the structure of a user's network affects their mental/emotional health. My work is situated somewhere in the overlap of human-computer interaction, network analysis, and artificial intelligence.
