Hi, I'm Anna!

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the University of Washington in Computer Science. I graduated from Cornell University with a BS in Computer Science in 2019 and worked as a software engineer at Amazon in Seattle.

I am broadly interested in social computing and human-computer interaction. My work explores how the networks of social and information systems can be leveraged to make people's lives healthy, productive, and fair, with applications to mental/emotional wellbeing, education and collaboration, and algorithmic fairness for disadvantaged groups.
